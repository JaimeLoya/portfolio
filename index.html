About Me


I have a degree in Geoinformatics from the Autonomous University of Ciudad Juárez and a Master's degree in Environmental Geography from the Environmental Geography Research Center of UNAM. I'm interested in data science and Geospatial BigData. I use Geographic Information Systems for spatial analysis and tools such as Google Earth Engine for processing satellite images. I'm interested in learning different programming languages, and although I know several, the ones that I use the most are Python and R. I began my professional career working with land cover and land use change, particularly deforestation. However, working conditions have allowed me to grow in other areas of knowledge, such as urban planning and biological control of diseases such as Dengue, Zika and Chikungunya.
